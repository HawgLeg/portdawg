CENTER OF BALANCE — CARGO / VEHICLE
Formula: (D×W per axle, summed) ÷ Gross Weight = CB from RDL
RDL = Reference Datum Line = front edge of vehicle or item.
Result = inches aft of RDL. Round .5+ up; drop .4 and below.
Ref: DTR 4500.9-R App P · FM 55-17 Ch 17
Cargo Type
Weigh forward and rear support points separately. Measure distance from front edge (RDL) to center of each support point.

Marking requirement (DTR App P): Mark both sides with masking tape in a "T". Write gross weight in the crossbar. Write "CB" in the vertical bar at the CB location. Use weather-resistant material. Trailers and prime movers marked separately even if connected.
